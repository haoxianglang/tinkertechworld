About Us

Building skills for a changing world through hands-on STEM learning.

We empower learners with hands-on projects, mentorship, and a community that celebrates curiosity and growth.

Our Story & Mission

We started Tinker Tech World to make STEM education practical, creative, and personal. Our programs are built around doing: designing, prototyping, testing, and learning from each iteration.

From first-time builders to advanced teams preparing for competitions, we provide the space, guidance, and culture that help people turn ideas into outcomes.

We help learners build confidence through real projects, mentorship, and collaboration. Our mission is to connect curiosity with technical skill and prepare students to solve real-world problems.

  • Project-based learning that connects ideas to outcomes.
  • Mentorship that supports growth, leadership, and resilience.
  • A community that celebrates creativity, teamwork, and curiosity.
